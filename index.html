Cat facts

Cat family

Cat is the closest pet to a human and have won the hearts of many

They are different species of cat that can be found across the world

siamese cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • Distinctive color points on the ears, face, paws, and tail
  • Blue almond-shaped eyes
  • Short coat with a sleek appearance

The Siamese cat is known for its vocal nature and social personality.

Maine Coon Cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • long- or medium-haired cat
  • Soft and Silky
  • eavily furred ears with extra long tufts of fur growing from inside

The Maine Coon Cat cat is known for its vocal nature and social personality.

Persian Cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • a round face and short muzzle
  • extremely long and thick coat, short legs, a wide head with ears set
  • can have virtually any colour or markings

The Persian cat is known for its vocal nature and social personality.
